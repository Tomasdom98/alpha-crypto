## üé® Content Guidelines

### Articles
- **Title**: Clear, engaging, SEO-friendly (50-60 characters)
- **Slug**: Auto-generated, but you can customize
- **Excerpt**: Compelling summary, max 200 characters
- **Content**: Detailed analysis, use formatting for readability
- **Category Options**:
  - **Stablecoins**: USDT, USDC, DAI analysis
  - **DeFi**: Protocol reviews, yield farming guides
  - **AI**: AI x Crypto projects
  - **Analysis**: Market analysis, technical analysis
  - **News**: Latest crypto news and updates

### Airdrops
- **Project Name**: Official project name
- **Logo URL**: Square image, 400x400px recommended
- **Steps**: Be specific, actionable, sequential
- **Estimated Reward**: Range format: "$500-2000" or "1000 TOKENS"
- **Difficulty**:
  - **Easy**: Simple social tasks, 5-10 mins
  - **Medium**: On-chain interactions, 20-30 mins
  - **Hard**: Complex tasks, development required
- **Status**: Set to "expired" after deadline passes

## üêõ Troubleshooting

**Content not showing on website?**

- Go to sanity.io, login, and check your projects list
